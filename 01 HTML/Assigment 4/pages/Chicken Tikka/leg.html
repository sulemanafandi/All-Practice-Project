Chicken Tikka - Leg

description: Chicken Tikka - Leg is a flavorful dish made from marinated chicken leg pieces that are grilled or baked to perfection. The chicken is typically marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices, giving it a tender texture and a rich, smoky flavor. It is often served with naan or rice, along with mint chutney and salad on the side.
