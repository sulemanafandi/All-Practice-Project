Cheese Pizza

description: Cheese Pizza is a classic Italian dish made with a thin crust topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and various toppings. It is baked in a hot oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Cheese Pizza is a versatile dish that can be customized with a variety of toppings, including vegetables, meats, and herbs.
