Kabab Burger

description: A Kabab Burger is a delicious sandwich that features a flavorful kabab patty made from ground meat (such as beef, chicken, or lamb) mixed with spices and herbs. The patty is typically grilled or pan-fried and served in a bun with various toppings like lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and sauces such as yogurt-based raita or spicy chutney.
