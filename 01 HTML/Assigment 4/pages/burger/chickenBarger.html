Chicken Burger

description: A chicken burger is a type of sandwich that features a cooked chicken patty, typically made from ground chicken meat. The patty is usually seasoned with various spices and herbs to enhance its flavor. It is served in a sliced bread roll or bun, often accompanied by toppings such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, onions, and condiments like mayonnaise, ketchup, or mustard. Chicken burgers can be grilled, fried, or baked, and they offer a leaner alternative to traditional beef burgers while still providing a delicious and satisfying meal option.
