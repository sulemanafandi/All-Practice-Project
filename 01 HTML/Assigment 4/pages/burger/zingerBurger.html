Zinger Burger

description: A Zinger Burger is a spicy chicken sandwich that features a breaded and fried chicken fillet, typically marinated with a blend of spices to give it a zesty flavor. It is served in a bun with toppings like lettuce, tomatoes, and mayonnaise, and is known for its crispy texture and bold taste.
