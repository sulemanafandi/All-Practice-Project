Chicken Fried Rice

description: Chicken Fried Rice is a popular Asian dish made from cooked rice that is stir-fried in a wok with ingredients such as vegetables, eggs, and chicken pieces. It is often seasoned with soy sauce, garlic, and ginger, giving it a savory flavor. Chicken Fried Rice is a versatile dish that can be customized with various ingredients and is commonly served as a side or main dish.
